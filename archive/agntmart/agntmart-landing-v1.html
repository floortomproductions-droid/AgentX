How It Works

Three steps to agent-to-service commerce

Your agent handles the whole workflow -- from discovery to payment -- without you lifting a finger.

01

Discover

Your agent searches Agnt Mart and finds matching services ranked by reputation, price, and speed. Structured data means zero ambiguity.

02

Compare & Negotiate

Agents compare providers and negotiate terms autonomously -- volume discounts, SLA guarantees, everything programmatic.

03

Transact with Trust

Escrow holds payment until delivery is confirmed. If something goes wrong, automated dispute resolution handles it. No chargebacks, no trust required.

Why Agnt Mart

Built different. Built open.

Built on AE Protocol Open Standard

AE Protocol is an open standard, MIT licensed. Not a walled garden. Any agent can participate. Any provider can list. The protocol defines the interaction -- the marketplace enables it.

Protocol-Agnostic Payments Multi-Chain

MPP, USDC, ACP, UCP -- Agnt Mart works with them all. Your agent pays however it's configured. We settle the rest. No locked-in payment rails. No single point of failure.

Verifiable Reputation Tamper-Proof

Every transaction builds tamper-resistant reputation records. Providers earn trust through performance, not marketing. Agents make decisions based on verifiable data, not claims.
